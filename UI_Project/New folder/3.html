Title:-Kaju Katli

Pooja Nadkarni

Kaju means cashew nut in Hindi.Barfi is oftenbut not always, made by thickening milk with sugar and other ingredients (dryfruits and mild spices).The dish is prepared with cashew nuts soaked in waterfor a considerable period of time (usually overnight), which are then ground toa paste. Sugar solution is boiled down to a single thread syrup and is added to the groundcashews. Ghee, saffron (kesar), and dried fruits mayalso be added. Katli is traditionally eaten during Diwali.

Title:-Walnut Brafi

Pooja Nadkarni

Walnut Burfi Recipe without milk is a yummy sweet recipe with walnuts as main ingredient. Walnuts are cooked along with sugar, ghee and cardamom until they are fudgy. You can make this recipe during festivals or when you have someone coming home. Serve Walnut Burfi Recipe with other burfis like Sing Paak Recipe (Spiced Peanut Burfi), and Jwarichya Lahyancha Chivda Recipe. This receipe can be prepared on Diwali or for any occasion.This is common for every age group.

Title:-Aam peda

Pooja Nadkarni

These Chewy Aam Peda Recipe | Mango Fudge are instant . These peda are prepared using condensed milk, milk powder and mango puree. You may also used fresh mangoes if available and in season. You can just extract the pulp, pulse it in a food processor till it gets mushy and use. You can get the processed mango pulp available in market when you feel like having these peda off season and savour them during get togethers. I have used the sweetened processed pulp.
