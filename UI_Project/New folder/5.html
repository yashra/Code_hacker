About Flavours...

Home Flavours was born in the enterprising thought of Yashraj when the first outlet took shape at Punjab. This was the beginning Over the years, the sweet aroma of Home Flavours spread in Punjab and slowly it wafted through the entire region.Come here to savour the dishes that can flatter your plates yet keep up the authentic flavours and aroma of age old Indian delicacies, dishes where purest of ingredients are used to give you a royal taste like none other, dishes so delightful in appearance and taste, you would like to have more n more. We at home flavours have always welcomed innovative ideas but to keep the taste intact has always been our priority. This has resulted in a relationship of trust and warmth and an all time world wide demand for some of our exotic sweets relished by people from all walks of life. 

How It Works ?

Every user in Home Flavours can also be a seller by creating their Home Sweets. The Home Seller can register to our site and sell the hand made sweets and earn money also he can order the already present sweets with a discount.This site has a benefit of giving money after delivering of sweets at your doorsteps.
